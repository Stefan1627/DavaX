### Formulas

**Current monthly labor cost**
`volume × (avg minutes / 60) × hourly cost`

**Monthly labor savings**
`current monthly labor cost × automatable share × adoption rate`

**Monthly quality savings**
`volume × current error rate × cost per error × expected reduction`

**Total monthly benefit**
`labor savings + quality savings + conservative revenue uplift`

**Monthly net value**
`total monthly benefit - monthly operating cost`

**Year-1 ROI**
`((monthly net value × 12) - one-time implementation cost) / one-time implementation cost`

**Payback period (months)**
`one-time implementation cost / monthly net value`
